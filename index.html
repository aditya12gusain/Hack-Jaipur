Jaipur - Cultural Diversity with a Royal Touch

Jaipur is one of the well known tours destinations in and is known for its rich heritage and culture. The city was an important center of the ancient Rajput dynasties. The ideal time to undertaking Jaipur tours is between the months of October and March. The weather in this part of the year is pleasant and dry. The city experiences hot and dry summers and mild and cool winters. The sky in the winter season is clear and the weather is charming. For charming Tours to Jaipur, travelers should make travel trips to the notable tourist attractions in Jaipur. In addition to the scenic beauty, there are plenty of historical and cultural specimens as well as wonderful scenic areas like parks and gardens. All these points of interest are popular points of sightseeing in Jaipur. One can avail of various types of guided tours to these places. The Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jaigarh Palace, The Albert Palace Museum, Lakshmi-Narayan Temple, The Jal Mahal, Rambagh Palace, Amber Fort, Chandra Mahal, Moti Doongri Temple, Akshardham Temple and so on. These places are visited by thousands of tourists from all over the world. Jaipur tours also consist of trips to the well known dining and shopping hot spots. There are wide shopping options in the city. The local shops sell wide range of crafts and artifacts while foreign made products are available in the supermarkets. Dining is one of the popular things to do in Jaipur. Travelers can have a taste of mouth watering local, Rajasthani and Indian delicacies from the regional and local dining spots. Excursion trips are very popular among Jaipur tours. Travelers can embark on excursion trips to the idyllic villages, woodlands and other beautiful surroundings in and around the city. Plenty of guided tours to Jaipur are available for the travelers. The tours range from Luxury India Tours to economy. There are weekend tours as well as week long tours. Travelers can get plenty of useful information from the tourist office and various other travel agencies.
